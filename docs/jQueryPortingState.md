Additional methods
------------------

phpQuery features many additional methods comparing to jQuery:

-   htmlOuter()
-   xml()
-   xmlOuter()
-   markup()
-   markupOuter()
-   getString()
-   reverse()
-   contentsUnwrap()
-   switchWith()
-   all from [PHPSupport](PHPSupport.md)
-   all from [Basic](Basic.md)
-   all from [MultiDocumentSupport](MultiDocumentSupport.md)
